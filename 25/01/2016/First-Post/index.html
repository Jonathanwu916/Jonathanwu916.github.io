Hexo - Fastest blog framework

I enjoy reading blogs. Sometimes, I got stuck on coding and the answer was found on someone’s blog instead of StackOverFlow.
So, an idea come up to my mind - why don’t I make a blog to share my experience?
After much searching and trying, there is one blog framework came into my sight - Hexo. It base on NodeJs, and allows you to deploy on Github. The most important thing is it was built up by a Taiwanese guy, which makes me feel intimate :grin:

How to modify , write and deploy it ?

You can go to [hexo configuration] to see the whole setting ways. I think there is no need to specify here.

Generally, when I want to post a new article there are only 4 lines of code need to type in GitBash

Let me explain what do they mean.

  • $ hexo post [layout]<title> means post a new article name by title.
  • $ hexo g means once you finish writing your blog, you type this for generating the files.
  • $ hexo s means you can test your blog on the local server.
  • $ hexo d means deploy your blog to your github pages.

Broadly speaking, hexo is a quite powerful and lightweight framework. Base on nodejs means it won’t become slow after many articles been post.
